Summer 2017

Galileo Summer Quest Staff (2017).

I worked as an summer intern at Galileo Summer Quest during the summer of 2017. Galileo is designed to help mentor young kids in different "majors" from a variety of topics such as 3D printing, video game programming, and digital design for the web. As a summer intern, I had the opportunity to assist campers with their assignments in a major of my choice. In one week of camp, I helped out campers in the 3D printing major troubleshoot problems in their programs and suggested ideas to help improve their models. For example, a camper and I spent time talking about the plane that he had designed and what improvements could be made to make it more effective in flight.
