Cello

I enjoy playing the cello, both in and outside of school. I'm the principal cellist at Cupertino High School's Chamber Orchestra, where I lead the cello section. My responsibilities include working with my team to help improve overall musicality and craftsmanship; I frequently hold small-group practice sessions, where I work with the cellists to help them learn the notes in the music and play at a higher standard.

Since 2010, I have been taking private cello lessons that have helped me develop my playing style. Additionally, I have received the Certificate of Merit (CM) Level 4, Level 5 and Level 6 from the Music Teacher's Association of California (MTAC).
